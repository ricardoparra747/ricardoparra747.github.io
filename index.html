About

I create computer programs to solve problems, automate stuff or process data. I do that by composing simple elements into robust systems, by collaborating to find a ubiquitous language, by looking for abstractions and patterns, by creating relevant tests and by iterating towards solution.

Proud husband of Ula and father of Simona. Trying to find my place in Białystok, Poland. I search for rhythms and patterns around me, in nature, music and art. From time to time I can be found learning how to fly, losing myself in a drumming circle, firing ceramics with my wife or taking pictures of local street art.
